About the Event

More Than a Concert

Ignite the Mic is a community-driven event that merges the arts and music with supporting cancer research. The event unites people, enabling them to support children and families going through incredibly hard times while battling cancer.

Through live performances, Ignite the Mic creates a deeply supportive atmosphere where entertainment serves purpose — reminding us that even small acts can make a big impact. Every dollar raised goes directly to helping children and families receive life-changing treatment at St. Jude Children's Research Hospital.

Ignite the Mic was founded to use the power of creativity, community, and compassion to make a meaningful difference. We chose to incorporate music and the arts because creative expression has a unique ability to bring people together, inspire empathy, and encourage action in a way that resonates deeply.
