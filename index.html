About the Event

Ignite the Mic is a fundraiser dedicated to helping families afford treatment at St. Jude Children's Hospital. Every dollar raised goes directly toward ensuring no family has to worry about the cost of care while their child fights for their life.

Join us for a morning/afternoon/night of live music and giving back to those who need it.
