About the Event

More Than a Concert

Ignite the Mic is a community driven event which merges the arts and music with supporting cancer research. The event unites people together, enabling them to support children and families going through incredibly hard times while battling cancer.

Through live performances, Ignite the Mic creates an incredibly supporting atmosphere where entertainment supports purpose, reminding people that even the small things you do can make a big impact. Every dollar raised goes directly to helping children and families receive life changing treatment, working towards the goal of enabling families to focus on their child's recovery without worrying about the cost of lifesaving care.

Our Mission

Why We Do This

Ignite the Mic was founded to use the power of creativity, community, and compassion to make a meaningful difference in the lives of children and families facing cancer. We chose to incorporate music, performance, and the arts because creative expression has a unique ability to bring people together, inspire empathy, and encourage action in a way that resonates deeply with others. Our mission is to remind the community that even small acts of support can create a lasting impact.
